About Me

My Background

My name is Kaiser Zhang, and I am a sociology student at New York University. I grew up in Fuyang, a city in Anhui Province, China. Moving to New York for college has allowed me to experience a new culture and meet people from many different backgrounds. As a sociology student, I am interested in understanding how society, culture, and inequality shape people's lives.

My Interests

Outside of school, I enjoy fitness training, traveling, and learning about cars. I spend a lot of time in the gym and enjoy setting personal goals. I also enjoy exploring different cultures through travel and meeting new people. In addition, I am interested in social issues and enjoy learning about how people interact with one another in different societies.
